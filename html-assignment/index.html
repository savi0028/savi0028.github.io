About Me

My name is Raven Saville, I am currently a student at Algonquin College. I am in the Interactive Media Design Program, this is my 1st year and I am enjoying it so far. I chose this program because I believe it will allow me to persue most of my creative endeavours, personally and as a career.

I have many creative endeavours and projects that I hope to create one day. Short-term I would like to create my own brand and all the media that goes along with it, like the color swatches, logo, website design etc. Long-term I have dreams of illustrating a book, creating an app, designing product packaging and many more. Ultimately I want to be an artist in as many ways possible, and I think thats what's great about this field/program in particular.
